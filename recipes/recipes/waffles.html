Waffles

About Waffles

The waffles are really amazing, it taste so damn good and we get a delight feeling while having them.

The crunch and taste which spreads all around the mouth is one of the mesmerizing experiences

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 4 tspn baking powder
  • 1/4 tspn salt
  • 1/2 tspn vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat waffle iron. Beat eggs in large bowl with hand beater until fluffy. Beat in flour, milk, vegetable oil, sugar, baking powder, salt and vanilla, just until smooth.
  2. Spray preheated waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray. Pour mix onto hot waffle iron. Cook untill goldren brown. Serve hot.
